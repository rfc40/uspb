Who we are

Formed in 19?? by....

We are a friendly non competing Gala day band. Based in Uphall Station with practise nights on thursday evenings (19:00-21:00). Always in the need for new members, both drummers and pipers so please feel free to come along

Currently we play mostly for local Gala days. Happy to entertain offers/suggestions for other occasions
